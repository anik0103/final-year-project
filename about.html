About Us

At Near & Dear, we believe that every celebration is made sweeter with the perfect cake. Founded with a passion for creating delightful confections, our mission is to bring joy and sweetness to your special moments. Whether it's a birthday, wedding, anniversary, or any other occasion, we craft cakes that not only look stunning but taste heavenly.

Our Story

Near & Dear started as a small family-owned bakery with a dream to spread happiness through our baked creations. Over the years, our dedication to quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction has allowed us to grow into a beloved name in the community. Every cake we create is a labor of love, crafted with the finest ingredients and utmost care.

Experienced Team

At Near & Dear, our team is the secret ingredient that makes our cakes extraordinary. Each member brings their unique skills, passion, and creativity to the table, ensuring that every cake we create is a masterpiece. Get to know the dedicated professionals who turn your sweet dreams into reality.
